Professional Career & Education

2013 - Now

Additional Superintendent of Police

Bangladesh Police

I joined the Bangladesh Police as an Assistant Superintendent of Police (ASP) through the 30th BCS in 2013. I was promoted to Senior ASP in 2016 and further advanced to the rank of Additional Superintendent of Police (Additional SP) in 2018. Throughout my career, I have served in various districts and vital locations across Bangladesh, where I successfully managed law enforcement duties, coordinated public safety operations, and contributed to maintaining peace and order. My experience has honed my leadership, strategic planning, and crisis management skills, enabling me to effectively address complex challenges in law enforcement.
